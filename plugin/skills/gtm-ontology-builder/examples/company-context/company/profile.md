# Acme Analytics

## Company snapshot

Acme Analytics is a fictional B2B software company that helps digital commerce
organizations understand customer behavior and activate first-party data. Its
products turn fragmented behavioral and transaction data into analysis and
audience workflows that commercial teams can use without maintaining a custom
analytics stack.

- **Website:** `https://acme-analytics.example`
- **Business model:** subscription software with annual and usage-based plans
- **Primary region:** Europe
- **Delivery:** cloud software, remote onboarding, and partner-assisted services
- **Company stage:** growth-stage vendor with a specialized product portfolio
