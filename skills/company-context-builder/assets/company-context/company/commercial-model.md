# Commercial model

## Revenue model

Describe how the company earns revenue and which commercial principles apply to
all product groups. Keep offer-specific packaging and prices in product files.

## Shared commercial rules

Record currency, taxes, contract duration, renewal, quote validity, payment terms,
usage treatment, onboarding rules, and the precedence of signed terms when known.

## Discount policy

State allowed discount bands, required approvers, non-standard terms, and prohibited
trade-offs. Do not infer policy from individual historical deals.

## Entry offers

Describe assessments, pilots, trials, or other entry mechanisms, including what
they do and do not commit either party to provide.

## Commercial boundaries

Record prohibited claims, guarantees, data uses, liabilities, and terms that belong
only in executed agreements.
