# Operating model

## Functional ownership

| Function | Company-wide responsibility |
|---|---|
| Unknown | Confirm the responsible functions and their durable ownership boundaries. |

Product-group artifacts may name a primary commercial or delivery role but must
not silently redefine company-wide ownership.

## Customer delivery model

Describe the ordered responsibilities from qualification through technical fit,
onboarding, validation, adoption, support, and renewal. Name both company and
customer responsibilities.

## Support model

Record support channels, coverage, response commitments, escalation boundaries,
and any product-specific variation.

## Operating boundaries

State which responsibilities remain with the customer and which services the
company does not provide.
