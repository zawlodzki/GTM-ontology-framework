# Commercial model

## Revenue model

Acme Analytics sells subscription software on annual contracts. Product plans
combine a platform fee with usage allowances appropriate to the product group.
Product files define their own packaging, included usage, and indicative prices;
this artifact defines the policies shared across the portfolio.

## Shared commercial rules

- Prices are stated in EUR and exclude taxes.
- Standard contracts run for 12 months and renew annually.
- Quotes remain valid for 30 calendar days unless the quote states otherwise.
- Standard payment terms are 30 days from invoice.
- Usage above the contracted allowance is billed according to the product's rate card.
- Onboarding may be mandatory when implementation or data-model work is required.
- Product-specific commitments override this document only when written in the order form.

## Discount policy

Sales may offer up to a 5% discount for annual prepayment without additional
approval. Larger discounts, non-standard liability terms, or free implementation
require approval from the Head of Revenue and Finance Lead. Discounts must not be
exchanged for undocumented scope reductions.

## Entry offers

Prospective customers may use a no-cost fit assessment to confirm product group,
data prerequisites, and implementation complexity. The assessment is not a data
audit, solution design, or binding implementation estimate.

## Commercial boundaries

Acme Analytics does not sell customer data, guarantee a specific revenue lift, or
accept responsibility for decisions made solely from automated recommendations.
Detailed security, data-processing, and service-level terms belong in executed
contracts rather than this static context.
